RocketSim

Enhancing the iOS Simulator

Compare designs, right in the simulator. Create recordings with touches, trim and export them into MP4 or GIF and share them anywhere using drag & drop.

And that's not all. Organise and launch Universal Links (deeplinks) with ease.

Terms of Use

Last updated: October 21st, 2021.

RocketSim enhances the iOS simulator with functionalities like recording into GIF or MP4, comparing designs, and opening universal links.

The app is build as a side-project by Antoine van der Lee who tries his very best to keep the app as up to date as possible. However, it could be that you're running into bugs that aren't directly solved. We hope you understand!

If you run into any issues, it's best to report them on GitHub.
