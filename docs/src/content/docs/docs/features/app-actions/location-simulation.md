---
title: "Location Simulation"
description: "Simulate GPS locations and freeway routes on the iOS Simulator. Test your app at any coordinate or run predefined driving and cycling scenarios."
---

Testing your app at different locations requires you to step in a car and bring your MacBook anywhere. This is time-consuming and not optimized for developing apps quickly.

RocketSim solves this by providing several ways of simulating locations. You can:

- Run specific scenarios:
  ![Location simulation scenario options including freeway drives and city runs](./location-simulation/cleanshot_2025-01-21_at_14.15.222x.png)
- Select a specific location:

![Custom location picker with latitude and longitude input](./location-simulation/cleanshot_2023-11-01_at_14.27.182x.png)

- Or configure often-used locations and routes using App Actions:
  ![Route simulation with GPX file import](./location-simulation/cleanshot_2023-11-01_at_14.29.002x.png)

For more details, I encourage you to read my article [\*\*Location Simulation in Xcode’s Simulator](https://www.avanderlee.com/workflow/location-simulation-xcode-simulator/).\*\*
