After every screenshot or recording, a floating thumbnail appears in the bottom-right corner of your screen. It's the fastest way to share, save, or edit your captures.

## Available actions

Use the thumbnail buttons to **Copy** to clipboard, **Save** (hold Cmd and click for Save As...), **Trim** video (scissors icon — recordings only), or **Delete** (trash icon).

## Drag and drop

The real power is drag-and-drop. Drag the thumbnail directly into Slack, GitHub, iMessage, App Store Connect, or any other destination. No need to find the file in Finder first.

## Preview and dismiss

Hover over the thumbnail and press Spacebar for a quick preview. Swipe the thumbnail to dismiss it.
