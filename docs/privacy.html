RocketSim Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how RocketSim collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Data Storage

RocketSim operates by storing all data locally on your device. We do not collect or store any of your data on external servers.

Build Insights syncs your scheme, project file name (e.g., MyProject.xcodeproj), and build durations to provide Team Insights. Additionally, machine metadata is synced to allow benchmarking by machine (e.g., determining which machine setup results in the best build duration). This metadata is generic and combined with a one-directional hash of the hardware machine UDID.

Tracking Services

RocketSim may utilize tracking services for analytics and crash reporting. We use Amplitude for anonymous analytics and Xcode for crash reports. This data helps us improve RocketSim's performance and user experience.

Email Lists

Users have the option to opt-in for our email lists to receive product updates. These updates may include information about new features, trial reminders, special offers, and other relevant news related to RocketSim. You can manage your email preferences at any time.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rocketsim.app.
